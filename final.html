Traffic Jam!

The goal of the game is for you to reach the end in the smallest amount of moves possible.
How to play? You are controlling the red car, and making it move, you need to click the right and left arrow keys.
To make the other vehicles move out of your way in order to escape, just click on them in the direction that you would like them to move.
For example, on the first level, if you wanted the green car to move to the left, you would click on its green square, which is the most in the left direction.
